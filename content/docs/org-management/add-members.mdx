## Adding Members to Your Organization

Owners and Managers can add members directly by email.

1. Navigate to the **Dashboard**
2. Select your organization from the **dropdown** at the top of the sidebar
3. Click **Members** in the sidebar
4. Enter the **email address** of the person you want to add
5. Click **Add Member**

The new member will be added with the **Member** role by default. You can [change their role](/docs/org-management/manage-roles) afterward.

> **Note:** The person must already have a Paladin account. If they haven't registered yet, ask them to sign up first, or they can [request to join](/docs/organizations/join-organization) your organization themselves.
