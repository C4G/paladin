## Map View

The Dashboard centers on an interactive Google Map showing your farms (green markers) and active disaster requests (red markers). The map auto-centers on your farms. If you have no farms registered yet, the map falls back to your current location (if location sharing is enabled) or a broad view of the United States.

> **Tip:** Your browser will prompt you to allow location sharing the first time you open the Dashboard. Allowing it helps the map center on nearby requests and farms.

- **Pan and zoom** to discover nearby requests and community farms
- Click any **marker** to see details or contact the owner
- Use **"All Requests"** or **"All Farms"** links at the top to zoom out and show everything

## Sidebar Tabs

The left sidebar has three tabs:

| Tab | Shows |
|-----|-------|
| **Yours** | Your own requests, filterable by Active / Inactive / All |
| **Members** | Active requests from your organization members' farms |
| **Nearby** | Nearby active requests visible on the current view of the map (with count badge) |

Click any request to open its detail panel with farm info, responders, and actions.

## Organization Switching

If you belong to multiple organizations, use the **dropdown** at the top of the left sidebar to switch between them. This changes which members' requests appear in the **Members** tab and allows owners/managers to manageme members in the sidebar.

From the sidebar you can also access:
- **Members** — view and manage org members (Owners/Managers can contact them via email here as well)
- **Join Requests** — handle pending join requests (Owners/Managers only)
- **Contact Owner** — reach out to the owner of the organization
