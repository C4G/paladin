## Disaster Resources

Admins can manage the disaster resources shown on the public **Resources** page.

### Adding a Resource

1. Navigate to **Resources** in the navigation bar at the top of the page
2. Click the **Add Resource** button
3. Fill in the **name**, **URL link**, and optional **description**
4. Click **Save**

### Editing a Resource

1. Find the resource in the list
2. Click the **Edit** button on the resource card
3. Update the fields and click **Save**

### Deleting a Resource

1. Click the **Delete** button on the resource card
2. Confirm the deletion when prompted
