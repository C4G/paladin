## Donating

You can support Paladin Farm & Ranch through PayPal donations. The donate option appears:
- On the **landing page** via the "Get Involved" section
- After completing **registration** (a donate dialog appears automatically)


### Monthly Subscription

1. Click **Donate**
2. Choose the **Subscribe** option to set up a recurring monthly donation through PayPal

> **Note:** An active monthly subscription is required for regular users to create disaster requests. Admins and Staff are exempt from this requirement.
