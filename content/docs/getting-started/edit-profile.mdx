## How to Edit Your Profile

1. Select your **profile icon** in the top right of the screen (the circle showing your Google account picture)
2. From the menu that appears, select **Edit Profile**
3. Navigate through the tabs to change any information you'd like to update
4. You may also add, update, and delete other farms if you have more than one
5. When complete, click the **Save Changes** button at the bottom of the screen

> **Note:** Only one farm may be modified, added, or deleted at a time.

## Adding a New Farm

From the profile editing page, click the dropdown in the top left, then click the **Add Farm** button. Then, fill in the details just like during registration.

## Deleting a Farm

Again from the profile editing page, click the dropdown in the top left. Select the farm you want to remove and click the **Delete Farm** button. You'll be asked to confirm before the farm is permanently deleted.
