## How to Register

1. Click **Sign In** in the top right of the screen
2. Click **Sign in with Google**
3. Enter your email address
4. Enter your password if prompted
5. On the Registration page, fill in the following tabs:
   - **Profile** — your name, phone number, email, and bio
   - **Farm Details** — farm name, address, location on the map, acreage, gates
   - **Crops** — list your crops and acreage (or select "No Crops")
   - **Livestock** — list your animals and count (or select "No Livestock")
   - **Equipment** — list available equipment (or select "No Equipment")
   - **Emergency Needs** — list any standing emergency needs (or select "No Needs")

After completing registration, you'll be redirected to the Dashboard.
