## How to Request a New Organization

1. Navigate to the **Organizations** page using the **Orgs** link in the navigation bar at the top of the page
2. Click the **Create Organization** tab
3. Enter a **name** for your organization
4. Enter a brief **description** explaining the organization's purpose
5. Click **Submit Request**

Your request will be reviewed by a platform administrator. You'll be notified by email when it's approved or rejected.

Once approved, you'll automatically be set as the **Owner** of the new organization and can begin inviting members.

> **Note:** You can only have one pending organization request at a time.
