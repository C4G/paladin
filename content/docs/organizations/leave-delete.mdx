---
title: Leave or Delete an Organization
description: How to leave an org or delete one as an admin
---

## Leaving an Organization

1. Navigate to the **Organizations** page
2. In the **My Organizations** tab, click the **…** (three-dot) menu on the organization card
3. Click **Leave Organization**

> **Note:** If you are the last Owner, you must first transfer ownership by [changing another member's role](/docs/organizations/manage-organization) to Owner before you can leave. Alternatively, you can choose to delete the organization.

## Deleting an Organization

1. Navigate to the **Organizations** page
2. In the **My Organizations** tab, click the **…** (three-dot) menu on the organization card
3. Click **Delete Organization**
4. Confirm when prompted — this permanently removes the organization and all memberships
