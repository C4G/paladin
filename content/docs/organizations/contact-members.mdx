## Contacting a Farm Owner

From the Dashboard map, you can contact a farm owner when they have an active request visible to you.

1. Click on a **request marker** on the map
2. In the info popup, click **Contact Owner**
3. Enter your message
4. Click **Send**

The farm owner will receive your message via email.

> **Note:** If there is no active request, you will only see the farm name and city/state on the map.

## Contacting an Organization Owner

1. Navigate to the **Organizations** page
2. Find the organization in the **My Organizations** tab
3. Click the **Contact Owner** option on the organization card
4. Enter your message and send

The organization's owner will receive your message via email.

## Emailing Organization Members

Admins, Owners, and Managers can email members directly from the Dashboard.

1. Navigate to the **Dashboard**
2. Select your organization from the dropdown
3. Click **Members** in the sidebar
4. Click the **email icon** next to a member's email address to open your mail client

## Emailing a User (Admin Only)

1. Click **Users** in the navigation bar at the top of the page
2. Find the user in the data table
3. Click the **email icon** next to their email address to open your mail client
