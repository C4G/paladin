Welcome to the Paladin Farm & Ranch user manual. Use the sidebar to navigate to a specific topic.

## Getting Started

- [Registration](/docs/getting-started/registration) — Create your account and register your first farm
- [Edit Profile](/docs/getting-started/edit-profile) — Update your profile and farm information
- [Light and Dark Mode](/docs/getting-started/light-dark-mode) — Switch between display themes
- [Donations](/docs/getting-started/donations) — Support Paladin through PayPal
- [Notifications](/docs/getting-started/notifications) — Push and email notification settings

## Dashboard

- [Dashboard Overview](/docs/dashboard/overview) — Map, sidebar tabs, and organization switching
- [Create an Emergency Request](/docs/dashboard/create-request) — Report a disaster on your farm
- [View Requests](/docs/dashboard/view-your-requests) — Check on your own and other farmers' requests
- [Respond to an Open Request](/docs/dashboard/respond-to-request) — Offer help to another farm
- [Close Your Open Request](/docs/dashboard/close-request) — Mark a request as resolved

## Organizations

- [Find and Join an Organization](/docs/organizations/join-organization) — Browse and request to join organizations
- [Create an Organization](/docs/organizations/create-organization) — Request a new organization
- [Manage Organization Members](/docs/organizations/manage-organization) — Add members, handle join requests, assign roles
- [Leave or Delete an Organization](/docs/organizations/leave-delete) — Leave an org or delete one (Admin)
- [Contact Members](/docs/organizations/contact-members) — Reach out to farm owners and org members

## Admin

- [Manage Users](/docs/admin/manage-users) — View/edit all users, change roles, export CSV
- [Manage Disaster Resources](/docs/admin/manage-resources) — Add, edit, and delete disaster resources
- [Review Organization Requests](/docs/admin/review-org-requests) — Approve or reject new organization requests
